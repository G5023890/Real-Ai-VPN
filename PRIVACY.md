# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Real Ai Router is a VPN client for iOS and macOS. This policy explains what data
the app handles and how it is used.

## Data Stored On Your Device

Real Ai Router stores imported VPN profiles, routing rules, app settings, and
local connection health history on your device. VPN profile secrets are stored in
Apple Keychain where supported by the app.

The app may keep local diagnostic details such as connection status, latency,
packet loss, handshake timing, failure counts, last check time, and Core ML
channel scoring results. These diagnostics are used to show connection health,
rank standby VPN channels, and support recovery or failover decisions.

## VPN Traffic

When you connect a VPN profile, network traffic is handled through Apple's
Network Extension framework and the VPN server configured by the profile you
import or select. Real Ai Router does not operate those third-party VPN servers.
The privacy practices of a VPN server or subscription provider are controlled by
that provider.

## Data Collection

Real Ai Router does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or
cross-app tracking. The app does not sell personal data.

Core ML scoring is performed on device using local connection quality signals.
The app does not require an account login for beta testing.

## User-Provided Profiles and Subscriptions

If you import a VPN configuration, subscription URL, or connection key, the app
uses that information to create and manage VPN connections. Only import profiles
from providers you trust.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the App Store Connect
support email associated with Real Ai Router.
